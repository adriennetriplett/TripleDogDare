ARTISTS

Meet our artists!

All of our artists are experienced, talented, and passionate about their art.

Learn about each one, and decide who might be the right artist for your new tattoo!

Kayla Johnson

Kayla is the owner of Triple Dog Dare Tattoo Studio. She has been tattooing for fourteen years, and specializes in intricate linework and geometric tattoos.

Sam Galley

Sam has been tattooing for nine years. Her specialties include illustrative, psychedelic, and surrealist tattoos, and she loves collaborating with her clients on innovative designs.

Daniella Rosario

Daniella specializes in portraiture and photo-realistic tattoos. She has been tattooing for seven years, and has won multiple awards for her work.

Jessa Houston

Jessa specializes in watercolor and neo-traditional tattoos. She has been tattooing for five years, and is also our resident piercing artist.
